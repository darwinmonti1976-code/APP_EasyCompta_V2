# EasyCompta — Store Listings (English)

## App Store

### Name (30 chars max)
EasyCompta

### Subtitle (30 chars max)
Voice-first AI finance tracker

### Description (4,000 chars max)
EasyCompta reinvents the way you manage your personal and business finances. Speak to record a transaction. Snap a receipt and let AI read it for you.

🎙 VOICE RECORDING
No more manual entry. Just say "I paid 45 francs at the grocery store" and EasyCompta instantly creates a transaction with the right amount, category, and type. Powered by OpenAI Whisper + GPT-4o.

📷 RECEIPT SCANNING
Photograph any receipt. EasyCompta reads the amount, category, and date in seconds using GPT-4o Vision, then lets you review before saving.

👨‍👩‍👧 SHARED WORKSPACES
Create a Family or Business workspace and invite members. Everyone adds their transactions, everyone sees the balance in real time. Instant sync powered by Supabase Realtime.

📊 BUDGETS & ANALYTICS
Set a budget per category (Groceries, Rent, Transport…) and instantly see whether you're on track. Category and monthly charts give you a clear view of your spending habits.

↻ RECURRING TRANSACTIONS
Rent, salary, subscriptions — declare them once and EasyCompta reminds you automatically at each due date.

📋 PDF & CSV EXPORT
Generate a monthly PDF report or export your data as CSV for your accountant or tax advisor.

🌙 DARK MODE
Clean interface in light or dark mode, with automatic system detection.

📱 HOME SCREEN WIDGET
Check your monthly balance at a glance without opening the app.

🔒 100% SECURE
Your data is encrypted and stored on Supabase (enterprise-grade PostgreSQL). Secure email authentication.
